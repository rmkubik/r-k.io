Feedback Night

Do you want to GET feedback on a game you're working on?

Do you want to GIVE feedback on in progress video games?

Come hang out with us and do either or both of these!

How it Works

We're going to have everyone interested in getting feedback on their game set up a station. Then, game owners will write down what the do and do NOT want feedback on. We'll provide these forms for folks to answer those questions on!

Every station will be given sticky notes and a marker. For each game, feedbacker givers will write down feedback on the points the demoer asked for. Leave one piece of feedback per sticky note! Try to leave at least one sticky note per game!

If you're bringing a game and want to bring a custom survey, thats a great idea too!
